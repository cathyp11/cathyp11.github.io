Spoon University at University of Michigan Chapter

10.2017 - Present

Spoon University is a global community of young influencers shaping the future of food. On our site, you can find the simplest recipes, the most obvious hacks you can’t believe you didn’t know, and the best restaurants around campus that you haven’t found yet. For many of us, this is the first time we’re navigating our campuses or our kitchens on our own, and Spoon University is here to simplify and celebrate that.

At University of Michigan, I joined Spoon University in junior year as a videographer. I created contents in form of visual stories on interesting topics such as the history of chocolate and spoon university events. After a year of videographer, I became a video director of our chapter and is continuing to lead a group of videographers.
