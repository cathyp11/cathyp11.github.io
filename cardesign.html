BMW M3 Concept Design

I wanted to investigate a concept design for the BMW M3 model in 2040 using one of my favorite automobile companies. I've designed a sleek and sporty car design from what the BMW M3 would seem like in the future by developing a major theme of tension, versatile, and daily purposes. Architecture, yachts, and apparel in the inspiration photos have a mix of sharp and smooth edges. I paid special attention to commuters who may enjoy their daily life when commuting in the city and scenic drives during their spare time with the focus on daily usage and versatile design.
