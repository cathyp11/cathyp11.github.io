Canvas Case Study

Canvas is a learning management system for Higher education and K-12. As students, we use this system everyday and often face challenges reaching our goals with the system. We wanted to look more in depth to see where improvements could be made. We’ve analyzed the mobile app of Canvas on its effectiveness of helping the students keep track of their coursework and organize assignments without accessing the web platform.
