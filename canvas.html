Canvas Case Study

UX Research

Canvas is a learning management system for Higher Education and K-12. As students, we use this system every day and often face challenges reaching our goals within system. We decided to look more in depth to see where improvements could be made. We analyzed the Canvas Mobile App on its effectiveness by tracking the students’ abilities to guide through the mobile app without accessing the web platform.
