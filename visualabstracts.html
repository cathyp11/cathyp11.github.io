What is a visual abstract?

A Visual Abstract acts as an infographic that quickly captures the core of an article or paper with visuals, essentially offering a visual version of the abstract. It simplifies the main concepts, enabling viewers to quickly understand the article's key points without reading the full text or traditional abstract. Though it omits the finer details of the article, its effectiveness is notable on social media platforms like Twitter, where it stands out to users scrolling through their feeds by summarizing essential insights.
