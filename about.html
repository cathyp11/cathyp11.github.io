About Me

Hi, I'm Yejin Park but I go by Cathy!

I'm a UI/UX Designer based in Boston, MA, originally from South Korea. I hold a Master's in Design Engineering from Harvard University and a Bachelor's in Information Science with a concentration in UI/UX Design and a minor in Applied Statistics from the University of Michigan. I'm passionate about creating intuitive and inclusive digital experiences that address real-world challenges. My work focuses on user-centered design and accessibility, aiming to improve people's lives through thoughtful and impactful design solutions. In my free time, I enjoy watching movies and video editing. When I travel, I create videos at the end of each trip to capture and remember the moments.
